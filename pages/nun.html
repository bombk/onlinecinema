The Nun is an upcoming American gothic supernatural horror film directed by Corin Hardy.[1] The screenplay by Gary Dauberman is from a story by James Wan and Dauberman.[2] It is a spin-off of 2016's The Conjuring 2 and the fifth installment in The Conjuring series, and stars Demián Bichir, Taissa Farmiga, and Jonas Bloquet. The film will be released in North America on September 7, 2018.
